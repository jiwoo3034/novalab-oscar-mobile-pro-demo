The Problem

The Mobile Gap in Primary Care

Thousands of Canadian clinicians rely on the desktop-first OSCAR EMR system daily. Being tethered to desktop terminals creates documentation delays during rounds and bedside care, driving after-hours charting and clinician burnout.

Our Solution

Bridging the Gap

Nova Lab's answer is OSCAR Mobile Pro, a secure, native, mobile-first EMR extension designed to give clinicians real-time access to patient records, appointment management, and secure messaging right at the point of care.

"The right data, in the right hands, at the right moment."

Key Features

Empowering Clinicians on the Go

Real-Time Sync and Offline Resilience

Caches data locally during network drops and auto-syncs with OSCAR EMR when connectivity is restored.

Structured SOAP Clinical Notes

Dedicated editor for S/O/A/P notes with auto-save, conflict merging, and offline queueing.

Lightning-Fast Patient Retrieval

Search by name, ID, or gender. Full tabbed patient records in under 2 seconds via cache-first strategy.

Secure Authentication

OAuth 2.0 and JWT login with optional biometric support (Face ID and fingerprint).

Appointment Management

Month, week, and day views tracking Scheduled, In-Progress, and Completed transitions.

Technology Stack

Modern Full-Stack Architecture

  • Frontend: Component-based UI with React, Tailwind CSS, and Vite.
  • Backend: RESTful API powered by Node.js and Express.js.
  • Database: 14-table normalized SQLite schema for clinical data integrity.
  • Testing: 16 Vitest suites, all passing.
  • Auth: OAuth 2.0 and JWT with optional biometric unlock.
